    Overview

    With so much of our personal and professional information stored online, it's crucial that companies take steps to protect their systems from hackers and cybercriminals. Thankfully, there are a number of platforms and individuals who are dedicated to finding vulnerabilities in popular software and hardware products. Here are some success stories of platforms finding vulnerabilities in different known companies:

    Bugcrowd

    This is a crowdsourced platform where individuals can submit vulnerabilities they find in different companies. Bugcrowd has helped companies such as Atlassian, Airbnb, and Dropbox to find and fix vulnerabilities in their systems. For example, in 2018, a Bugcrowd researcher discovered a vulnerability in the Zoom video conferencing software that could have allowed a hacker to spy on users' conversation

    Google Project Zero

    This is a team of security researchers at Google who are focused on finding vulnerabilities in popular software and hardware products. The team has found vulnerabilities in companies such as Apple, Microsoft, and Adobe. In 2019, for example, a Project Zero researcher discovered a vulnerability in the Apple iMessage app that could have allowed a hacker to take over a user's phone simply by sending a text message.

    HackerOne

    This is another crowdsourced platform where individuals can submit vulnerabilities they find in different companies. HackerOne has helped companies such as Spotify, Airbnb, and GitHub to find and fix vulnerabilities in their systems. In 2018, for example, a HackerOne researcher discovered a vulnerability in the Shopify e-commerce platform that could have allowed a hacker to access sensitive customer information.

    Tavis Ormandy

    Tavis is a security researcher who works at Google Project Zero. He has found vulnerabilities in companies such as Microsoft, Symantec, and McAfee. In 2017, for example, Ormandy discovered a vulnerability in the Microsoft Malware Protection Engine that could have allowed a hacker to take control of a user's computer simply by sending an email.

    Sam Curry

    Sam is a security researcher who found a vulnerability in Instagram that allowed him to delete any photo on the platform. He reported the vulnerability to Instagram and was awarded a $6,000 bounty. This is just one example of how individuals can play an important role in improving the security of popular platforms.
